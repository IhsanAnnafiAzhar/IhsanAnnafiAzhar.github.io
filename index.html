Menentukan volume grafik \(y= \frac{x^2}{\pi}\) dengan batas \(x=4\) dan \(y=0\)

  • Subtitusikan \(y=0\) ke \(y= \frac{x^2}{\pi}\) \[0=\frac{x^2}{\pi}\] \[0=x^2\] \[0=x\] \[x=0\]

  • Subtitusikan ke dalam rumus \(V=\pi \int_b^a (f(x))^2 dx\) dengan \(a=4\) dan \(b=0\) \[V=\pi \int_0^4 (\frac{x^2}{\pi})^2 dx\] \[V=\pi \int_0^4 \frac{x^4}{\pi^2} dx\] \[V=\pi \frac{1}{\pi^2} \int_0^4 x^4 dx\] \[V=\pi \frac{1}{\pi^2} \int_0^4 x^4 dx\] \[V=\frac{1}{\pi} \int_0^4 x^4 dx\] \[V=\frac{1}{\pi} \Bigg[\frac{1}{5}x^5\Bigg]_0^4 \] \[V=\frac{1}{\pi} \Bigg[\Big(\frac{1}{5}4^5\Big)-\Big(\frac{1}{5}0^4\Big)\Bigg]\] \[V=\frac{1}{\pi} \Bigg[\Big(\frac{1}{5}1024\Big)-0\Bigg]\] \[V=\frac{1}{\pi} \Big(\frac{1}{5}1024\Big)\] \[V=\frac{1}{\pi} \Big(\frac{1024}{5}\Big)\] \[V=\frac{1024}{5\pi}\]

    • Jadi volumenya adalah \(\frac{1024}{5\pi}\) satuan volume
